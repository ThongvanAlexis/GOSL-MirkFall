**Fix-loop strategies (user chooses at Task 1):**

**Strategy A — per-finding atomic commit (Phase 02 default):**
- One commit per §3 fix row
- Each commit gated on CI green before next push
- Maximum bisectability (`git bisect` lands on individual finding)
- Wall-clock cost: N findings × ~6 min CI cycle = ~N×6 min total
- Best when N ≤ 15 findings OR when bisect granularity matters

**Strategy B — batched fix loop (Phase 04 Plan 04-05 precedent):**
- Group related fixes into batches of ~3-5 commits
- Each batch gated on CI green before next batch is pushed
- Coarser bisectability (`git bisect` lands on batch)
- Wall-clock cost: M batches × ~10 min CI cycle = ~M×10 min total (where M ≈ N/4)
- Best when N > 15 findings OR when wall-clock matters more than bisect granularity
- Phase 04 Plan 04-05: 31 findings → 10 batches × ~10 min CI = ~100 min vs ~186 min per-finding (saved ~86 min)
